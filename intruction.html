How to Play Simon Says Game

1. Start The Game :

Click the "Start Game" button to begin.

2. Watch the Sequence:

The game will highlight a sequence of colors. Pay close attention to the order.

3. Repeat the Sequence:

Click the buttons in the same order as the highlighted sequence.

4. Advance Levels:

Each correct sequence will advance you to the next level, adding a new color to the sequence.

5. Game Over:

If you click the wrong button, the game will end, and your score will be displayed.
Click the "Start Game" button to restart and try again.

6. Highest Score:

Your highest score in the current session will be tracked and displayed.


Enjoy the game and challenge your memory skills!
